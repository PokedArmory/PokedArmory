About Me

My coding career started in 2018 (Over 5 years ago!) when I created my first Scratch game using a very simple type of coding called block coding. Since then I have coded in LUA, Javascript, C#, HTML, CSS, and more! You can see some of my games here.

During the covid lockdown I started learning to 3D model in Blender, and just like alomst everyone else I followed the Blender donut tutorial (You can view my donut here!). Since then I have created dozens of low poly models and art work in Blender.
